CIVIL ENGINEERING

Civil Engineering is one of the broadest and oldest of the engineering disciplines, extending across many technical specialties. Civil Engineers plan, design, and supervise the construction of facilities essential to modern life like space satellites and launching facilities, offshore structures, bridges, buildings, tunnels, highways, transit systems, dams, airports, harbors, water supply system and wastewater treatment plants.

SCOPE
  • Civil ENGG has lot of scope because which deals basic facilities of the people, civilization of country and basic infrastructure for IT sector.
VISION
  • To mould the students into successful civil engineers in their profession.
MISSION
  • To provide teaching learning process that prepares engineers to meet the needs of the construction industry.
  • To provide environment for self learning to meet the challenges in construction industries and inculcate team spirit and leadership qualities to succeed in professional carrier.
  • To install professional ethics and concerns for the environment for the benefits of society.

COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

Computer Science & Engineering involves the design and understanding of computational process and programming languages. Computer Science & ENGG is the building block for hardware and it is acting like soul of the hardware.

SCOPE
  • Computer Science & ENGG has lot of scope because human life is 100% was adopted with technologies and India is moving towards a better tomorrow which definitely includes IT Sector of the nation which has to be developed for a better economy.
VISION
  • The Department will offer, through best-in-class faculty and infrastructure, globally acceptable education in Computer Science and produce highly competent and value-based computer engineers
MISSION
  • Impart quality education with an emphasis on basic principles of Computer Science and Engineering
  • To provide ambience for self learning to meet the challenges of changing technology and inculcating team spirit and good communication skills to succeed in career
  • To install professional ethics and concerns of the environment for the benefit of stake holders.

ELECTRONICS & COMMUNICATIONS ENGINEERING

Electronics and Communication Engineering deals with the electronic devices and software applications. It is an interface of chip level hardware and information technology. Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) compiles in Communication Engineering and core Electronics Engineering

SCOPE
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering has lot scope because this is the backbone of the computer science department as well as IT sectors. Electronics and Communication Engineering is also mother of the science sector
VISION
  • To mould the students into excellent engineers in the electronics and communication industries.
MISSION
  • To provide teaching learning practices that prepares industry ready engineers.
  • To provide ambience for self learning to meet the challenges of changing technology and inculcating team spirit and good communication skills to succeed in career.
  • To instill professional ethics and concerns of the environment for the benefit of stake holders

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Mechanical engineering is a one of the popular branch especially in rural area peoples, which helps students/humans to understand the working mechanisms of heavy tools and machineries. Mechanical engineering is also provides the basic need of the human like vehicles, machineries etc

SCOPE
  • Mechanical ENGG deals with human needs like power sector, air conditioning sector etc. It is also backbone of the construction industry, automotive industry and aircraft industry.
VISION
  • To mould the students into professional engineers in mechanical and allied industries
MISSION
  • To provide teaching learning process that prepares the engineers to the needs of the industry and higher learning.
  • To provide environment for self learning to meet the challenges of changing technology and inculcating team spirit and good communication skills to succeed in professional career.
  • To instill professional ethics and concerns of the environment for the benefit of stake holders and community by and large.
